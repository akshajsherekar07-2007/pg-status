How StaySync Works

From browsing to booking, in six simple steps — all in real time.

Step 01

Browse Properties

Search PGs, hostels, and flats with live availability filters.

Step 02

Check Bed Availability

See real-time color-coded bed status — green, yellow, or red.

Step 03

Place a Hold

Request 1-7 day holds on vacant beds while you decide.

Step 04

Get Instant Updates

Real-time notifications on hold acceptance, expiry, or room changes.

Step 05

Visit & Confirm

Visit the property during your hold window. All agreements happen offline.

Step 06

Move In

Owner confirms booking after agreement. Bed turns red — it's yours!
